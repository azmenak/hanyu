# An Inclination to Casual Looting
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：你还有多少钱？
乙：五十。
甲：那是我的钱！给我。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 你还有多少钱？
    nǐ hái yǒu duōshǎo qián ？
    How much money do you have?

乙: 五十。
    wǔshí 。
    Fifty bucks.

甲: 那是我的钱！给我。
    nà shì wǒ de qián ！ gěi wǒ 。
    That's my money! Give it to me.
### Vocab
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] my
-还 (hái) [adverb] still
-有 (yǒu) [verb] to have
-多少 (duōshǎo) [question] how much
-钱 (qián) [noun] money
-五 (wǔ) [number] five
-十 (shí) [number] ten
-那 (nà) [determiner] that
-给 (gěi) [verb] to give
-时间 (shíjiān) [noun] time
