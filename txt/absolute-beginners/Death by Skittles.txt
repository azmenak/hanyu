# Death by Skittles
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：哦，彩虹糖！
甲：我吃一个。
甲：真好吃。
甲：谢谢啊！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 哦，彩虹糖！
    ò ， cǎihóngtáng ！
    Oh, Skittles!

甲: 我吃一个。
    wǒ chī yī ge 。
    I'll have one.

甲: 真好吃。
    zhēn hǎochī 。
    Really delicious.

甲: 谢谢啊！
    xièxiè ā ！
    Thanks!
### Vocab
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I
-我爱你 (wǒ ài nǐ) [phrase] I love you
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-他 (tā) [pronoun] he
-是 (shì) [verb] to be
-谁 (shéi) [question] who
-宝贝 (bǎobèi) [noun] baby
-不 (bù) [adverb] not
-认识 (rènshi) [verb] to recognize
-知道 (zhīdào) [verb] to know
-爱 (ài) [verb] to love
