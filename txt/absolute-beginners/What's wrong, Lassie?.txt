# What's wrong, Lassie?
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
狗：汪... 汪....
男人：怎么了，莱西？ 提米怎么了？
狗：汪汪汪....
男人：我的天哪，他掉到井里了？
狗：汪汪汪汪汪汪汪....
男人：马上要爆炸了？
狗：汪。
男人：快上车，我们赶快走。

### Pinyin and Translation

狗: 汪... 汪....
    wàng ... wàng ....
    Woof... woof....

男人: 怎么了，莱西？ 提米怎么了？
      zěnme le ， Láixī ？  Tímǐ zěnme le ？
      What's wrong Lassie? What happened to Timmy?

狗: 汪汪汪....
    wàng wàng wàng . . . .
    Bark, bark, bark....

男人: 我的天哪，他掉到井里了？
      wǒ de tiān na ， tā diàodào jǐng lǐ le ？
      My heavens, he fell into a well?

狗: 汪汪汪汪汪汪汪....
    wàng wàng wàng wàng wàng wàng wàng . . . .
    Bark bark bark bark bark bark....

男人: 马上要爆炸了？
      mǎshàng yào bàozhà le ？
      And it's going to blow up right away?

狗: 汪。
    wàng 。
    That's right.

男人: 快上车，我们赶快走。
      kuài shàngchē ， wǒmen gǎnkuài zǒu 。
      Get in the car, we're going at once.
### Vocab
-莱西 (Láixī) [name] Lassie
-拉稀 (lāxī) [verb] to have diarrhea
-汪 (wàng) [sound] bark
-狗 (gǒu) [noun] dog
-马上 (mǎshàng) [adverb] at once
-要 ... 了 (yào ... le) [structure] will
-掉 (diào) [verb] to fall
-走 (zǒu) [verb] to go
-爆炸 (bàozhà) [verb] to explode
-赶快 (gǎnkuài) [adverb] with all haste
-出去 (chūqù) [verb] to get out
-睡觉 (shuìjiào) [verb] to sleep
-我的天哪 (wǒ de tiān na) [phrase] my God
