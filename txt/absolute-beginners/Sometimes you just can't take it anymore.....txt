# Sometimes you just can't take it anymore....
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：停下。
甲：够了，够了。
甲：住手！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 停下。
    tíngxià 。
    Stop.

甲: 够了，够了。
    gòule ， gòule 。
    Enough, enough.

甲: 住手！
    zhù shǒu ！
    Stop it!
### Vocab
-请 (qǐng) [other] please
-停 (tíng) [verb] to stop
-停下 (tíngxià) [verb] to stop
-停车 (tíngchē) [phrase] stop the car
-不要 (bù yào) [verb] not to want
-松手 (sōngshǒu) [verb] to let go
-不用了 (bù yòng le) [phrase] there's no need
-够了 (gòu le) [phrase] enough
-住手 (zhùshǒu) [verb] stop!
-手 (shǒu) [noun] hand
