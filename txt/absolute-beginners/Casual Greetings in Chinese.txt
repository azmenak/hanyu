# Casual Greetings in Chinese
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：咳，我听说他....
乙：嘿，怎么样？
甲：呃....
乙：你们在说什么？
甲：没说什么，没说什么。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 咳，我听说他....
    hai ， wǒ tīngshuō tā . . . .
    Hey, I heard he....

乙: 嘿，怎么样？
    hei ， zěnmeyàng ？
    Hey, what's up?

甲: 呃....
    e . . . .
    Uhhh....

乙: 你们在说什么？
    nǐmen zài shuō shénme ？
    What are you guys saying?

甲: 没说什么，没说什么。
    méi shuō shénme ， méi shuō shénme 。
    Nothing, nothing at all.
### Vocab
-你好 (nǐhǎo) [phrase] hello
-你好吗 (nǐhǎoma) [phrase] how do you do
-怎么样 (zěnmeyàng) [phrase] what is up
-最近怎么样 (zuìjìn zěnmeyàng) [phrase] how are things lately
-还好吗 (hái hǎo ma) [phrase] how are things
-还行 (hái xíng) [phrase] okay
-挺好的 (tǐng hǎo de) [phrase] pretty good
-嘿 (hei) [exclamation] hey
-嗨 (hai) [exclamation] hi
-哎 (ai) [exclamation] eh
-听 (tīng) [verb] to hear
-说 (shuō) [verb] to say
-在 (zài) [adverb] currently
-没 (méi) [adverb] not
