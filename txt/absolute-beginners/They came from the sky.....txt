# They came from the sky....
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：疼，疼疼疼疼疼....
乙：哪儿疼啊？这儿？
甲：嗷！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 疼，疼疼疼疼疼....
    téng ， téng téng téng téng téng . . . .
    Oh, it hurts....

乙: 哪儿疼啊？这儿？
    nǎr téng ā ？ zhèr ？
    Where does it hurt, here?

甲: 嗷！
    ào ！
    Ow!
### Vocab
-有 (yǒu) [verb] to have
-死 (sǐ) [verb] to die
-打 (dǎ) [verb] to hit
-吃 (chī) [verb] to eat
-做 (zuò) [verb] to do
-了 (le) [particle] (past tense)
-没 (méi) [adverb] not
-蚊子 (wénzi) [noun] mosquito
-吃了吗 (chī le ma) [phrase] have you eaten
-等一下 (děng yī xià) [phrase] wait a moment
-等一等 (děng yī děng) [phrase] wait a bit
-等一会儿 (děng yī huìr) [phrase] wait a while
