# The Third Tone
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
你好
我很好
酒很好
我很忙
没有水
中国人很好
你的英文很好
他的中文真好

### Pinyin and Translation

你好
nǐhǎo
Hello

我很好
wǒ hěn hǎo
I am very well

酒很好
jiǔ hěn hǎo
The wine is good

我很忙
wǒ hěn máng
I am very busy

没有水
méiyǒu shuǐ
There is no water

中国人很好
Zhōngguórén hěn hǎo
Chinese people are nice

你的英文很好
nǐ de yīngwén hěn hǎo
Your english is pretty good

他的中文真好
tā de zhōngwén zhēn hǎo
His Chinese is really good
### Vocab
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-别 (bié) [other] don't
-动 (dòng) [verb] to move
-事 (shì) [noun] problem
-没事 (méishì) [phrase] there's no problem
-有事 (yǒushì) [phrase] there's a problem
-别管我 (bié guǎn wǒ) [phrase] don't nag me
-别说 (bié shuō) [phrase] don't talk
-别笑 (bié xiào) [phrase] don't laugh
-没 (méi) [adverb] not, there is not
