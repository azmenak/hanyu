# The Unsuccessful Haggle
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
男：谁放屁啦？
女：不是我。
男：很臭啊。
女：是你吧！

### Pinyin and Translation

男: 谁放屁啦？
    shéi fàngpì la ？
    Who farted?

女: 不是我。
    bù shì wǒ 。
    It wasn't me.

男: 很臭啊。
    hěn chòu a 。
    That really smells.

女: 是你吧！
    shì nǐ ba ！
    It was you!
### Vocab
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I, me
-今天 (jīntiān) [time, noun] today
-怎么样 (zěnmeyàng) [phrase] how is it like
-不 (bù) [adverb] not, no
-不怎么样 (bù zěnmeyàng) [phrase] not to be too good
-为什么 (wèishénme) [question] why
-因为 (yīnwèi) [conjunction] because
-熬夜 (áoyè) [verb] to stay up really late
-很 (hěn) [adverb] very
-累 (lèi) [adjective] physically exhausted
