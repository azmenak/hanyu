# Want money?
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：你要钱吗？
甲：要啊！
乙：不给。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 你要钱吗？
    nǐ yào qián ma ？
    You want money?

甲: 要啊！
    yào ā ！
    Yes!

乙: 不给。
    bù gěi 。
    I won't give you any.
### Vocab
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I; me
-不 (bù) [adverb] not
-要 (yào) [verb] to want
-给 (gěi) [verb] to give
-钱 (qián) [noun] money
-水 (shuǐ) [noun] water
-吗 (ma) [particle] (question)
