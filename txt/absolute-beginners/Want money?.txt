# Want money?
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：几点了？
乙：两点。
甲：天哪！晚了，晚了！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 几点了？
    jǐ diǎn le ？
    What time is it?

乙: 两点。
    liǎng diǎn 。
    Two o'clock.

甲: 天哪！晚了，晚了！
    tiānna ！ wǎn le ， wǎn le ！
    Good heavens! It's late!
### Vocab
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I; me
-不 (bù) [adverb] not
-要 (yào) [verb] to want
-给 (gěi) [verb] to give
-钱 (qián) [noun] money
-水 (shuǐ) [noun] water
-吗 (ma) [particle] (question)
