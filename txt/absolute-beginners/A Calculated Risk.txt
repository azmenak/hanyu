### Vocab
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-生气 (shēngqì) [verb] to get angry
-吃饭 (chīfàn) [verb] to eat food
-上学 (shàngxué) [verb] to go to school
-上班 (shàngbān) [verb] to go to work
-睡觉 (shuìjiào) [verb] to sleep
-上网 (shàngwǎng) [verb] to go online
-什么 (shénme) [question] what
-这样啊 (zhè yàng ā) [phrase] it's this way
