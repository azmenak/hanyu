# Alternatives to the X-Ray
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：疼，疼疼疼疼疼....
乙：哪儿疼啊？这儿？
甲：嗷！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 疼，疼疼疼疼疼....
    téng ， téng téng téng téng téng . . . .
    Oh, it hurts....

乙: 哪儿疼啊？这儿？
    nǎr téng ā ？ zhèr ？
    Where does it hurt, here?

甲: 嗷！
    ào ！
    Ow!
### Vocab
-疼 (téng) [adjective] painful
-舒服 (shūfu) [adjective] comfortable
-好 (hǎo) [adjective] good
-哪儿 (nǎr) [pronoun] where
-那儿 (nàr) [pronoun] there
-这儿 (zhèr) [pronoun] here
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I; me
-很 (hěn) [adverb] very
