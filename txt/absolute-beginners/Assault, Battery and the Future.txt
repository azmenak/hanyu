# Assault, Battery and the Future
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：我会打你的。
乙：你敢打我？
甲：我真的真的会打你的。
乙：你真的敢打我？

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 我会打你的。
    wǒ huì dǎ nǐ de 。
    I will hit you.

乙: 你敢打我？
    nǐ gǎn dǎ wǒ ？
    You dare to hit me?

甲: 我真的真的会打你的。
    wǒ zhēnde zhēnde huì dǎ nǐ de 。
    I'm really really going to hit you.

乙: 你真的敢打我？
    nǐ zhēnde gǎn dǎ wǒ ？
    You really dare to hit me?
### Vocab
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-会 (huì) [verb] will
-敢 (gǎn) [verb] to dare
-打 (dǎ) [verb] to hit
-吃 (chī) [verb] to eat
-给 (gěi) [verb] to give
-用 (yòng) [verb] to use
-说 (shuō) [verb] to speak
-真的 (zhēnde) [adverb] really
-中文 (zhōngwén) [noun] Chinese
-不会 (bùhuì) [verb] won't
-学习 (xuéxí) [verb] to study
-告诉 (gàosù) [verb] to tell
