# Mamma Mia
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：姚葳，姚葳，你在吗？问你个问题！
乙：她今天很忙。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 姚葳，姚葳，你在吗？问你个问题！
    Yáo Wēi ， Yáo Wēi ， nǐ zài ma ？ wèn nǐ ge wèntí ！
    Echo, Echo, are you there? I've got a question to ask!

乙: 她今天很忙。
    tā jīntiān hěn máng 。
    She's very busy today.
### Vocab
-她 (tā) [pronoun] she
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-在 (zài) [verb] to be at
-吗 (ma) [particle] question marker
-问 (wèn) [verb] to ask
-问题 (wèntí) [noun] question
-今天 (jīntiān) [noun] today
-很 (hěn) [adverb] very
-忙 (máng) [adjective] busy
-好 (hǎo) [adjective] good
-累 (lèi) [adjective] tired
