# Parking the Car
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：先生，请系好安全套。
乙：啊？你说什么？
甲：安全带，安全带。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 先生，请系好安全套。
    xiānsheng ， qǐng jìhǎo ānquántào 。
    Mister. Please put on your condom.

乙: 啊？你说什么？
    a ？ nǐ shuō shénme ？
    What? What did you say?

甲: 安全带，安全带。
    ānquándài ， ānquándài 。
    Seatbelt, seatbelt....
### Vocab
-卫生间 (wèishēngjiān) [noun] bathroom
-洗手间 (xǐshǒujiān) [noun] washroom
-手纸 (shǒuzhǐ) [noun] toilet paper
-洗手 (xǐshǒu) [verb] to wash hands
-用完了 (yòngwán le) [verb phrase] to be used up
-洗完了 (xǐwán le) [verb phrase] to be done washing
-看完了 (kànwán le) [verb phrase] to be done looking
-吃完了 (chīwán le) [verb phrase] to be done eating
