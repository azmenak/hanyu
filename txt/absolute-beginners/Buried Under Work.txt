# Buried Under Work
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：累吗？
乙：嗯，我很累。
甲：你该睡觉。
乙：我还有工作。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 累吗？
    lèi ma ？
    Are you tired?

乙: 嗯，我很累。
    ng ， wǒ hěn lèi 。
    Yes, I'm very tired.

甲: 你该睡觉。
    nǐ gāi shuìjiào 。
    You should go to sleep.

乙: 我还有工作。
    wǒ hái yǒu gōngzuò 。
    I still have work to do.
### Vocab
-很 (hěn) [adverb] very
-累 (lèi) [adjective] tired
-吗 (ma) [question marker] yes/no
-嗯 (ng) [interjection] hmmm
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-该 (gāi) [auxiliary verb] should
-睡觉 (shuìjiào) [verb] to go to bed
-还 (hái) [adverb] also
-有 (yǒu) [verb] to have
-工作 (gōngzuò) [noun] work
