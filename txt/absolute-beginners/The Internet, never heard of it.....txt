# The Internet, never heard of it....
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
一：你舒服吗？
二：我很舒服，为什么？啊！
一：开玩笑。

### Pinyin and Translation

一: 你舒服吗？
    nǐ shūfu ma ？
    Are you comfortable?

二: 我很舒服，为什么？啊！
    wǒ hěn shūfu ， wèishénme ？ a ！
    I'm very comfortable, why? Arg!

一: 开玩笑。
    kāi wánxiào 。
    Just kidding.
### Vocab
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-在 (zài) [preposition] at
-哪儿 (nǎr) [question] where
-什么 (shénme) [question] what
-网上 (wǎngshàng) [noun] online
-因特网 (yīntèwǎng) [noun] Internet
-没听说过 (méi tīngshuō guo) [phrase] not to have heard of
-没听见 (méi tīngjiàn) [phrase] not to have heard
-没听懂 (méi tīngdǒng) [phrase] not to have understood
