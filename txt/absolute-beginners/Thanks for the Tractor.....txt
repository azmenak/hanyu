### Pinyin and Translation

中国行
Zhōngguó xíng
China is ok.

十一人
shíyī rén
11 people

中国人说中文
Zhōngguórén shuō zhōngwén
Chinese people speak Chinese

英国人说英文
Yīngguórén shuō yīngwén
People from England speak English

多花钱
duō huāqián
Spend more money

他行
tā xíng
He is ok

他很忙
tā hěn máng
He is very busy

中国人花钱
Zhōngguórén huāqián
Chinese people spend money

别烦人
bié fánrén
Don't be such a nuisance
