# Thanks for the Tractor....
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：你别动!
乙：没事!
甲：别动!
乙：没事!
甲：别动!
乙：没....

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 你别动!
    nǐ bié dòng !
    Don't move, you!

乙: 没事!
    méishì !
    Don't worry about it!

甲: 别动!
    bié dòng !
    Don't move!

乙: 没事!
    méishì !
    There's nothing wrong!

甲: 别动!
    bié dòng !
    Don't move!

乙: 没....
    méi ....
    There's no....
### Vocab
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-别 (bié) [other] don't
-动 (dòng) [verb] to move
-事 (shì) [noun] problem
-没事 (méishì) [phrase] there's no problem
-有事 (yǒushì) [phrase] there's a problem
-别管我 (bié guǎn wǒ) [phrase] don't nag me
-别说 (bié shuō) [phrase] don't talk
-别笑 (bié xiào) [phrase] don't laugh
-没 (méi) [adverb] not, there is not
