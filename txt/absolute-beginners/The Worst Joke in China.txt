# The Worst Joke in China
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：哎呀，这服务太差了。这儿谁管呢？
乙：饺子。
甲：谁？
乙：饺子馆呢！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 哎呀，这服务太差了。这儿谁管呢？
    aiya ， zhè fúwù tài chà le 。 zhèr shéi guǎn ne ？
    Great Scott! The service here is awful. Who is in charge!

乙: 饺子。
    jiǎozi 。
    Dumplings.

甲: 谁？
    shéi ？
    Who?

乙: 饺子馆呢！
    jiǎoziguǎn ne ！
    The dumpling restaurant!
### Vocab
-谁 (shéi) [pronoun] who
-管 (guǎn) [verb] to manage
-饺子 (jiǎozi) [noun] dumpling
-馆 (guǎn) [noun] establishment
-饭馆 (fànguǎn) [noun] restaurant
-面馆 (miànguǎn) [noun] noodle shop
-饺子馆 (jiǎoziguǎn) [noun] dumpling restaurant
-宾馆 (bīnguǎn) [noun] hotel
-饭 (fàn) [noun] rice
-面 (miàn) [noun] noodles
-服务员 (fúwùyuán) [noun] waitress
-服务 (fúwù) [noun] service
