# An Unholy Smell
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
男：谁放屁啦？
女：不是我。
男：很臭啊。
女：是你吧！

### Pinyin and Translation

男: 谁放屁啦？
    shéi fàngpì la ？
    Who farted?

女: 不是我。
    bù shì wǒ 。
    It wasn't me.

男: 很臭啊。
    hěn chòu a 。
    That really smells.

女: 是你吧！
    shì nǐ ba ！
    It was you!
### Vocab
-谁 (shéi) [question] who
-放屁 (fàngpì) [verb] to fart
-不 (bù) [adverb] not
-是 (shì) [verb] to be
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] me
-很 (hěn) [adverb] very
-臭 (chòu) [adjective] smelly
-吧 (ba) [particle] (suggestion or guess)
-放心 (fàngxīn) [verb] to relax, be at ease
-放手 (fàngshòu) [verb] to release
-屁话 (pìhuà) [phrase] nonsense
