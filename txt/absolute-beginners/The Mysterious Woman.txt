# The Mysterious Woman
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
男：你是谁？
女：我是你的老婆！

### Pinyin and Translation

男: 你是谁？
    nǐ shì shéi ？
    Who are you?

女: 我是你的老婆！
    wǒ shì nǐ de lǎopo ！
    I'm your wife!
### Vocab
-你是谁 (nǐ shì shéi) [phrase] who are you
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-他 (tā) [pronoun] he
-她 (tā) [pronoun] she
-是 (shì) [verb] to be
-谁 (shéi) [question] who
-我的 (wǒ de) [phrase] my
-你的 (nǐ de) [phrase] your
-他的 (tā de) [phrase] his
-她的 (tā de) [phrase] her
-老婆 (lǎopo) [noun] wife
-老公 (lǎogōng) [noun] husband
