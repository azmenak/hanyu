# What Time Is It?
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：几点了？
乙：两点。
甲：天哪！晚了，晚了！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 几点了？
    jǐ diǎn le ？
    What time is it?

乙: 两点。
    liǎng diǎn 。
    Two o'clock.

甲: 天哪！晚了，晚了！
    tiānna ！ wǎn le ， wǎn le ！
    Good heavens! It's late!
### Vocab
-几点了 (jǐ diǎn le) [phrase] what time is it
-几 (jǐ) [question] how many
-点 (diǎn) [noun] hour
-一 (yī) [number] one
-二 (èr) [number] two
-三 (sān) [number] three
-五 (wǔ) [number] five
-两 (liǎng) [number] two
-四 (sì) [number] four
-天哪 (tiānna) [exclamation] heavens
-晚 (wǎn) [adjective] late
