# What Time Is It?
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
男：你真漂亮。
女：你真帅。
男：你真聪明。
女：你真... 帅。

### Pinyin and Translation

男: 你真漂亮。
    nǐ zhēn piàoliang 。
    You're really pretty.

女: 你真帅。
    nǐ zhēn shuài 。
    You're really handsome.

男: 你真聪明。
    nǐ zhēn cōngming 。
    You're really smart.

女: 你真... 帅。
    nǐ zhēn ... shuài 。
    You're really... handsome.
### Vocab
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I
-真 (zhēn) [adverb] truly
-好 (hǎo) [adverb] really
-漂亮 (piàoliang) [adjective] pretty
-聪明 (cōngming) [adjective] intelligent
-帅 (shuài) [adjective] handsome
-傻 (shǎ) [adjective] foolish
-好 (hǎo) [adjective] good
-美 (měi) [adjective] beautiful
-性感 (xìnggǎn) [adjective] sexy
-幸福 (xìngfú) [adjective] fortunate
