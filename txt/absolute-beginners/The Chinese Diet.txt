# The Chinese Diet
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：你真能吃，菜又没了。
乙：开玩笑！我在减肥。
甲：减肥还吃那么多？
乙：我没吃米饭。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 你真能吃，菜又没了。
    nǐ zhēn néng chī ， cài yòu méi le 。
    You can really eat, all of the food's gone again.

乙: 开玩笑！我在减肥。
    kāiwánxiào ！ wǒ zài jiǎnféi 。
    You're kidding! I'm on a diet.

甲: 减肥还吃那么多？
    jiǎnféi hái chī nàme duō ？
    You're on a diet and you're still eating that much?

乙: 我没吃米饭。
    wǒ méi chī mǐfàn 。
    I didn't have any rice.
### Vocab
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I
-真 (zhēn) [adverb] really
-能吃 (néngchī) [adjective] gluttonous
-又 (yòu) [adverb] once again
-开玩笑 (kāiwánxiào) [phrase] you're kidding
-在 (zài) [preposition] currently
-减肥 (jiǎnféi) [verb] to diet
-还 (hái) [adverb] still
-米饭 (mǐfàn) [noun] cooked rice
-没了 (méi le) [verb phrase] to be gone
