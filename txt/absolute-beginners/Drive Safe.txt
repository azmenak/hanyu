# Drive Safe
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：先生，请系好安全套。
乙：啊？你说什么？
甲：安全带，安全带。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 先生，请系好安全套。
    xiānsheng ， qǐng jìhǎo ānquántào 。
    Mister. Please put on your condom.

乙: 啊？你说什么？
    a ？ nǐ shuō shénme ？
    What? What did you say?

甲: 安全带，安全带。
    ānquándài ， ānquándài 。
    Seatbelt, seatbelt....
### Vocab
-先生 (xiānsheng) [noun] Mister
-请 (qǐng) [verb] please
-系好 (jìhǎo) [verb] to fasten
-安全 (ānquán) [adjective] safe
-安全套 (ānquántào) [noun] condom
-安全带 (ānquándài) [noun] seat belt
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-说 (shuō) [verb] to say
-什么 (shénme) [question] what
