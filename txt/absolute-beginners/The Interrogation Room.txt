# The Interrogation Room
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：一，二....
乙：停，我说。我告诉你，我都告诉你！
丙：别，别告诉他！
甲：你看，他懂事儿。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 一，二....
    yī ， èr . . . .
    One, two....

乙: 停，我说。我告诉你，我都告诉你！
    tíng ， wǒ shuō 。 wǒ gàosu nǐ ， wǒ dōu gàosu nǐ ！
    Stop, I'll talk. I'll tell you. I'll tell you everything!

丙: 别，别告诉他！
    bié ， bié gàosu tā ！
    Don't, don't tell him!

甲: 你看，他懂事儿。
    nǐ kàn ， tā dǒngshìr 。
    You see? He gets it.
### Vocab
-一 (yī) [number] one
-二 (èr) [number] two
-三 (sān) [number] three
-停 (tíng) [verb] to stop
-说 (shuō) [verb] to speak
-告诉 (gàosu) [verb] to tell
-都 (dōu) [adverb] all
-别 (bié) [adverb] don't
-懂事儿 (dǒngshìr) [phrase] to understand things
-请 (qǐng) [phrase] please
-中文 (zhōngwén) [noun] Chinese
-英文 (yīngwén) [noun] English
