# Where is the Washroom
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
女的：洗手间在哪儿？
男的：这儿没有。
女的：那哪儿有？
男的：这附近都没有洗手间呀。

### Pinyin and Translation

女的: 洗手间在哪儿？
      xǐshǒujiān zài nǎr ？
      Where is the washroom?

男的: 这儿没有。
      zhèr méiyǒu 。
      There isn't any washroom here.

女的: 那哪儿有？
      nà nǎr yǒu ？
      So where is one?

男的: 这附近都没有洗手间呀。
      zhè fùjìn dōu méiyǒu xǐshǒujiān ya 。
      There aren't any washrooms anywhere around here.
### Vocab
-洗手间 (xǐshǒujiān) [noun] washroom
-你 (nǐ) [pronoun] you
-我 (wǒ) [pronoun] I
-在 (zài) [unknown] to be at
-哪儿 (nǎr) [question] where
-这儿 (zhèr) [noun] here
-那儿 (nàr) [noun] there
-有 (yǒu) [verb] to have
-没有 (méiyǒu) [verb] not to have
-手纸 (shǒuzhǐ) [noun] toilet paper
-你在哪儿 (nǐ zài nǎr) [phrase] where are you
-我在这儿 (wǒ zài zhèr) [phrase] I'm over here
