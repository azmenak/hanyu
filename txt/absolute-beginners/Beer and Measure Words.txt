# Beer and Measure Words
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
女的：洗手间在哪儿？
男的：这儿没有。
女的：那哪儿有？
男的：这附近都没有洗手间呀。

### Pinyin and Translation

女的: 洗手间在哪儿？
      xǐshǒujiān zài nǎr ？
      Where is the washroom?

男的: 这儿没有。
      zhèr méiyǒu 。
      There isn't any washroom here.

女的: 那哪儿有？
      nà nǎr yǒu ？
      So where is one?

男的: 这附近都没有洗手间呀。
      zhè fùjìn dōu méiyǒu xǐshǒujiān ya 。
      There aren't any washrooms anywhere around here.
### Vocab
-买单 (mǎidān) [verb] to order the bill
-服务员 (fúwùyuán) [noun] waiter
-埋单 (máidān) [verb] to order the bill (variant)
-马上 (mǎshàng) [adverb] immediately
-付账 (fùzhàng) [verb] to get the bill
-付钱 (fùqián) [verb] to pay money
-结账 (jiézhàng) [verb] to settle the bill
-算账 (suànzhàng) [verb] to order the bill
-打包 (dǎbāo) [verb] to take away
-多少 (duōshǎo) [question] how much
