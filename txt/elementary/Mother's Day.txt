# Mother's Day
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：Bunny, 你给你妈买礼物了吗？
乙：为什么？
甲：因为母亲节！
乙：哦，我忘了！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: Bunny, 你给你妈买礼物了吗？
    Bunny, nǐ gěi nǐ mā mǎi lǐwù le ma ？
    Bunny, did you buy a present for your mom?

乙: 为什么？
    wèishénme ？
    Why?

甲: 因为母亲节！
    yīnwèi mǔqīnjié ！
    Because of Mother's Day!

乙: 哦，我忘了！
    o ， wǒ wàng le ！
    Oh, I forgot!
### Vocab
-给 (gěi) [preposition] for
-妈 (mā) [noun] mom
-爸 (bà) [noun] dad
-礼物 (lǐwù) [noun] gift
-买 (mǎi) [verb] to buy
-为什么 (wèishénme) [question] why
-因为 (yīnwèi) [conjunction] because
-母亲节 (mǔqīnjié) [noun] Mothers Day
-忘 (wàng) [verb] to forget
