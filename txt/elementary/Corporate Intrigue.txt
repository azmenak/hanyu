# Corporate Intrigue
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
女：你被解雇了！
男：开玩笑！我是老板的儿子！
女：什么？我是老板的女儿！
男：这么说......咱们俩是姐弟......
女：天哪！这是什么公司！

### Pinyin and Translation

女: 你被解雇了！
    nǐ bèi jiěgù le ！
    You're being let go.

男: 开玩笑！我是老板的儿子！
    kāiwánxiào ！ wǒ shì lǎobǎn de érzi ！
    You've got to be kidding. I'm the boss' son.

女: 什么？我是老板的女儿！
    shénma ？ wǒ shì lǎobǎn de nǚér ！
    What? I'm the boss' daughter!

男: 这么说......咱们俩是姐弟......
    zhème shuō ...... zánmen liǎ shì jiědì ......
    That means we're brother and sister.

女: 天哪！这是什么公司！
    tiānna ！ zhè shì shénme gōngsī ！
    Good gracious! What kind of company is this!
### Vocab
-被 (bèi) [preposition] by
-解雇 (jiěgù) [verb] to be fired
-开玩笑 (kāiwánxiào) [phrase] you're kidding
-老板 (lǎobǎn) [noun] boss
-儿子 (érzi) [noun] son
-咱们 (zánmen) [pronoun] we
-女儿 (nǚér) [noun] daughter
-这么说 (zhème shuō) [unknown] this is to say
-俩 (liǎ) [noun] two
-姐弟 (jiědì) [noun phrase] older sister and younger brother
-天哪 (tiānna) [phrase] good gracious
-公司 (gōngsī) [noun] company
-姐姐 (jiějie) [noun] older sister
-妹妹 (mèimei) [noun] younger sister
-哥哥 (gēge) [noun] older brother
-弟弟 (dìdi) [noun] younger brother
