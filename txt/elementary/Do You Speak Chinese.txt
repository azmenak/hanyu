# Do You Speak Chinese
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：你会说中文吗？
乙：当然了，我是中国人。
甲：噢，我原来以为你是韩国人呢！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 你会说中文吗？
    nǐ huì shuō Zhōngwén ma ？
    Do you speak Chinese?

乙: 当然了，我是中国人。
    dāngrán le ， wǒ shì zhōngguórén 。
    Of course, I'm Chinese.

甲: 噢，我原来以为你是韩国人呢！
    o ， wǒ yuánlái yǐwéi nǐ shì hánguórén ne ！
    Oh, I thought you were Korean!
### Vocab
-会 (huì) [auxiliary verb] can
-当然 (dāngrán) [conjunction] of course
-中国人 (zhōngguórén) [noun] Chinese person
-噢 (o) [particle] Oh!
-原来 (yuánlái) [adverb] originally
-以为 (yǐwéi) [verb] to think mistakenly
-韩国人 (hánguórén) [noun] Korean person
-呢 (ne) [particle] (sound indicates slight surprise)
-会说话 (huì shuōhuà) [verb phrase] to be a smooth talker
-电脑 (diànnǎo) [noun] computer
