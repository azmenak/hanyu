# A Letter from the Cat
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：咱们的猫不再爱你了。它给你写的信。
乙：咱们的猫会写字儿？
甲：对，我一直在训练它。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 咱们的猫不再爱你了。它给你写的信。
    zánmen de māo bù zài ài nǐ le 。 tā gěi nǐ xiě de xìn 。
    Our cat no longer loves you. This is the letter it wrote you.

乙: 咱们的猫会写字儿？
    zánmen de māo huìxiě zìr ？
    Our cat can write?

甲: 对，我一直在训练它。
    duì ， wǒ yīzhí zài xùnliàn tā 。
    Yes. I've been training him for a while.
### Vocab
-咱们 (zánmen) [pronoun] we; us
-它 (tā) [pronoun] it
-一直 (yīzhí) [adverb] continuously
-不再 (bù zài) [adverb] no longer
-在 (zài) [adverb] currently
-写 (xiě) [verb] to write
-爱 (ài) [verb] to love
-训练 (xùnliàn) [verb] to train
-字儿 (zìr) [noun] characters
-猫 (māo) [noun] cat
-信 (xìn) [noun] letter
