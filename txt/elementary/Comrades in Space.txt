# Comrades in Space
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：我们在太空里经历了太多的冒险。
乙：是的船长，这是我此生最难忘的旅行！
甲：噢，太刺激了。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 我们在太空里经历了太多的冒险。
    wǒmen zài tàikōng lǐ jīnglì le tài duō de màoxiǎn 。
    We've experienced a lot of adventures in space.

乙: 是的船长，这是我此生最难忘的旅行！
    shì de chuánzhǎng ， zhè shì wǒ cǐshēng zuì nánwàng de lǚxíng ！
    Yes Captain, this has been the most unforgettable journey of my life.

甲: 噢，太刺激了。
    o ， tài cìji le 。
    Hmm... extremely stimulating.
### Vocab
-太空 (tàikōng) [noun] outer space
-同志 (tóngzhì) [noun] comrade; homosexual
-冒险 (màoxiǎn) [noun] dangerous adventure
-此生 (cǐshēng) [noun] in this life
-旅行 (lǚxíng) [noun] journey
-难忘 (nánwàng) [adjective] unforgettable
-刺激 (cìji) [adjective] stimulating
-受挫 (shòucuò) [adjective] frustrating
-无聊 (wúliáo) [adjective] boring
-委屈 (wěiqū) [adjective] unfairly wronged
-伤心 (shāngxīn) [adjective] wounded; hurt
-开心 (kāixīn) [adjective] happy
-吃惊 (chījīng) [adjective] startled
