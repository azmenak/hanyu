# The Carlsberg Man
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：酒保，四杯威士忌！
乙：哎，你别喝那么多威士忌吧。
甲：别管我，你不懂爱情！
丙：刚分手？想喝酒？要喝就喝嘉士伯！
甲：给我一杯嘉士伯。
乙：哎，这就对了！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 酒保，四杯威士忌！
    jiǔbǎo ， sì bēi wēishiji ！
    Bartender, four whiskeys!

乙: 哎，你别喝那么多威士忌吧。
    āi ， nǐ bié hē nàme duō wēishiji ba 。
    Hey, don't drink that much whiskey.

甲: 别管我，你不懂爱情！
    bié guǎn wǒ ， nǐ bù dǒng àiqíng ！
    Leave me alone, you don't understand love.

丙: 刚分手？想喝酒？要喝就喝嘉士伯！
    gāng fēnshǒu ？ xiǎng hē jiǔ ？ yào hē jiù hē jiāshìbó ！
    Just broke up? Feel like drinking? If you want a drink, drink Carlsberg!

甲: 给我一杯嘉士伯。
    gěi wǒ yī bēi jiāshìbó 。
    Give me a glass of Carlsberg.

乙: 哎，这就对了！
    ai ， zhè jiù duì le ！
    Hey, that's more like it!
### Vocab
-酒保 (jiǔbǎo) [noun] bartender
-威士忌 (wēishiji) [noun] whiskey
-嘉士伯 (jiāshìbó) [noun] Carlsberg
-别 (bié) [adverb] don't
-别管我 (bié guǎn wǒ) [phrase] leave me alone
-刚 (gāng) [adverb] recently
-刚刚 (gānggāng) [adverb] really recently
-刚才 (gāngcái) [noun] just now
-分手 (fēnshǒu) [verb] to split up
-就 (jiù) [adverb] precisely
-甭 (béng) [adverb] don't
