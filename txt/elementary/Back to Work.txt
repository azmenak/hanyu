# Back to Work
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：哎呀，坏了，老板在。
乙：不许聊天，回去工作。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 哎呀，坏了，老板在。
    āiya ， huài le ， lǎobǎn zài 。
    Oh no, dammit, the boss is here.

乙: 不许聊天，回去工作。
    bù xǔ liáotiān ， huíqu gōngzuò 。
    No chatting allowed, get back to work.
### Vocab
-哎呀 (āiya) [exclamation] good gracious
-坏了 (huài le) [phrase] it's bad
-糟了 (zāo le) [phrase] it's awful
-完了 (wán le) [phrase] I'm done for
-老板 (lǎobǎn) [noun] boss
-在 (zài) [verb] to be present
-不许 (bù xǔ) [verb] not to be allowed
-聊天 (liáotiān) [verb] to chat
-工作 (gōngzuò) [verb] to work
-回去 (huíqu) [verb] to return
-磨蹭 (móceng) [verb] to procrastinate
