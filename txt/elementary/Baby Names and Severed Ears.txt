# Baby Names and Severed Ears
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：你女儿真漂亮。
乙：哦，谢谢。
甲：她叫什么名字？
乙：没想好。
甲：那，她多大了？
乙：两三岁左右。
甲：两三岁了还没有取名字？
乙：我工作太忙了。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 你女儿真漂亮。
    nǐ nǚr zhēn piàoliang 。
    Your daughter is very pretty.

乙: 哦，谢谢。
    ò ， xièxie 。
    Oh, thanks.

甲: 她叫什么名字？
    tā jiào shénme míngzi ？
    What's her name?

乙: 没想好。
    méixiǎnghǎo 。
    Haven't thought of one yet.

甲: 那，她多大了？
    nà ， tā duō dà le ？
    So, how old is she?

乙: 两三岁左右。
    liǎng sān suì zuǒyòu 。
    Around two or three.

甲: 两三岁了还没有取名字？
    liǎng sān suì le hái méiyǒu qǔ míngzi ？
    She's two or three years old and you still haven't picked a name for her?

乙: 我工作太忙了。
    wǒ gōngzuò tài máng le 。
    I've been really busy at work.
### Vocab
-沒想好 (méixiǎnghǎo) [verb] not to have finished thinking
-没做好 (méizuòhǎo) [verb] not to have finished
-没说好 (méishuōhǎo) [verb] not to have agreed upon
-没定好 (méidìnghǎo) [verb] not to have set down
-取 (qǔ) [verb] to pick up
-取名字 (qǔ míngzi) [verb] to pick a name
-取钱 (qǔ qián) [verb] to take out money
-取签证 (qǔ qiānzhèng) [verb] to pick up a visa
-两三 (liǎng sān) [other] two or three
-岁 (suì) [noun] years
-左右 (zuǒyòu) [suffix] more or less
-馘 (guó) [verb] to cut off the left ear of a slain opponent
