# The Fourteenth Floor
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：水费这么多！
乙：哦，多少？
甲：六千多。
乙：哦，我最近经常洗澡。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 水费这么多！
    shuǐfèi zhème duō ！
    The water bill is so expensive!

乙: 哦，多少？
    o ， duōshǎo ？
    Oh, how much?

甲: 六千多。
    liùqiān duō 。
    More than six thousand.

乙: 哦，我最近经常洗澡。
    o ， wǒ zuìjìn jīngcháng xǐzǎo 。
    Oh, I've been showering a lot these days.
### Vocab
-随便 (suíbiàn) [phrase] whatever
-火锅 (huǒguō) [noun] hot pot
-水煮鱼 (shuǐzhǔyú) [noun] boiled fish
-上火 (shànghuǒ) [verb] to create internal heat
-去火 (qùhuǒ) [verb] to diminish internal heat
-辣 (là) [adjective] spicy
-究竟 (jiūjìng) [adverb] (adds emphasis to questions)
-什么 (shénme) [noun] anything
-哪 (nǎ) [noun] anywhere
-谁 (shéi) [noun] anyone
