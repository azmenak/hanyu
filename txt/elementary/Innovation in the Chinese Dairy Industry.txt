# Innovation in the Chinese Dairy Industry
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：这是你的内裤吗？
乙：嗯....
甲：你怎么放到冰箱里了？
乙：那个....
甲：我在酸奶瓶里找到的！
乙：你喝酸奶了吗？
甲：我受够了，我要分手！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 这是你的内裤吗？
    zhè shì nǐ de nèikù ma ？
    Is this your underwear?

乙: 嗯....
    en . . . .
    Um....

甲: 你怎么放到冰箱里了？
    nǐ zěnme fàngdào bīngxiāng lǐ le ？
    Why did you put it in the fridge?

乙: 那个....
    nà ge . . . .
    Well....

甲: 我在酸奶瓶里找到的！
    wǒ zài suānnǎi píng lǐ zhǎodào de ！
    I found it in the bottle of yoghurt!

乙: 你喝酸奶了吗？
    nǐ hē suānnǎi le ma ？
    Did you drink the yoghurt?

甲: 我受够了，我要分手！
    wǒ shòugòu le ， wǒ yào fēnshǒu ！
    I've had it, I want to break up.
### Vocab
-怎么 (zěnme) [question] how
-内裤 (nèikù) [noun] underwear
-冰箱 (bīngxiāng) [noun] refrigerator
-酸奶 (suānnǎi) [noun] yoghurt
-分手 (fēnshǒu) [verb] to split up
-受不了 (shòubùliǎo) [verb] to be unable to take it
-受够了 (shòugòu le) [verb] to have had enough
-非要 (fēiyào) [verb] to really have to
