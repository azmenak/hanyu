# Happy Easter
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：我给你买了一条围巾。
乙：真好看。
甲：是猫毛做的。你不是很喜欢猫吗？
乙：我对猫过敏....

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 我给你买了一条围巾。
    wǒ gěi nǐ mǎi le yī tiáo wéijīn 。
    I bought you a scarf.

乙: 真好看。
    zhēn hǎokàn 。
    It's really beautiful.

甲: 是猫毛做的。你不是很喜欢猫吗？
    shì māomáo zuò de 。 nǐ bù shì hěn xǐhuān māo ma ？
    It's made of cat hair. Don't you just love cats?

乙: 我对猫过敏....
    wǒ duì māo guòmǐn . . . .
    I'm allergic to cats....
### Vocab
-条 (tiáo) [measure word] long stripe
-给 (gěi) [coverb] for; to
-对 (duì) [coverb] towards
-围巾 (wéijīn) [noun] scarf
-毛 (máo) [noun] fur
-过敏 (guòmǐn) [verb] to be allergic
-感兴趣 (gǎn xìngqu) [verb] to be interested in
-满意 (mǎnyì) [verb] to be satisfied
-失望 (shīwàng) [verb] to be disappointed
-生气 (shēngqì) [verb] to be angry
-有信心 (yǒu xìnxīn) [verb] to have confidence
