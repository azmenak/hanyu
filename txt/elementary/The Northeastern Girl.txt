# The Northeastern Girl
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：这个怎么说？
甲：笑什么你？
乙：你刚跑出东北味来了。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 这个怎么说？
    zhège zěnme shuō ？
    How do you say this?

甲: 笑什么你？
    xiào shénme nǐ ？
    What are you laughing at?

乙: 你刚跑出东北味来了。
    nǐ gāng pǎochū dōngběiwèi lái le 。
    You just let slip a Northeastern accent.
### Vocab
-怎么说 (zěnme shuō) [phrase] how to say
-东北 (dōngběi) [noun] northeast
-刚 (gāng) [adverb] recently
-笑 (xiào) [verb] to laugh
-跑出 (pǎochū) [verb] to run
-说出 (shuōchū) [verb] to blurt out
-唱出 (chàngchū) [verb] to sing out
-想出 (xiǎngchū) [verb] to think of
-听出 (tīngchū) [verb] to hear
