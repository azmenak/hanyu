# The Shark Tank
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：今天下午你去打扫鲨鱼池。
乙：鲨鱼不吃人？
甲：对，所以我们把你打扮成一只海豹。
乙：那鲨鱼不吃海豹？
甲：你是海豹吗？

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 今天下午你去打扫鲨鱼池。
    jīntiān xiàwǔ nǐ qù dǎsǎo shāyú chí 。
    This afternoon you're going to clean the shark tank.

乙: 鲨鱼不吃人？
    shāyú bù chī rén ？
    Don't sharks eat people?

甲: 对，所以我们把你打扮成一只海豹。
    duì ， suǒyǐ wǒmen bǎ nǐ dǎbàn chéng yī zhī hǎibào 。
    Yes, which is why we'll be dressing you up as a seal.

乙: 那鲨鱼不吃海豹？
    nà shāyú bù chī hǎibào ？
    But, don't sharks eat seals?

甲: 你是海豹吗？
    nǐ shì hǎibào ma ？
    Are you a seal?
### Vocab
-打扫 (dǎsǎo) [verb] to clean
-池 (chí) [noun] pool
-缸 (gāng) [noun] tank
-所以 (suǒyǐ) [conjunction] therefore
-把 (bǎ) [verb] to take
-打扮 (dǎbàn) [verb] to dress up
-鲨鱼 (shāyú) [noun] shark
-海豹 (hǎibào) [noun] seal
-虾 (xiā) [noun] shrimp
-螃蟹 (pángxie) [noun] crab
-鱼 (yú) [noun] fish
-金鱼 (jīnyú) [noun] goldfish
