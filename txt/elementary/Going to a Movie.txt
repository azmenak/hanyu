# Going to a Movie
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：今天晚上有事吗?
乙：没事。
甲：那我们一起去看电影吧?
乙：好呀。

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 今天晚上有事吗?
    jīntiān wǎnshang yǒushì ma ?
    Got anything tonight?

乙: 没事。
    méishì 。
    Nope.

甲: 那我们一起去看电影吧?
    nà wǒmen yīqǐ qù kān diànyǐng ba ?
    Let's go watch a movie then, ok?

乙: 好呀。
    hǎo ya 。
    Ok.
### Vocab
-今天 (jīntiān) [noun] today
-晚上 (wǎnshang) [noun] evening
-有事 (yǒushì) [verb phrase] to be occupied
-吗 (ma) [particle] 
-没事 (méishì) [verb] not to have anything
-那 (nà) [conjunction] in that case
-一起 (yīqǐ) [preposition] together
-电影 (diànyǐng) [noun] movie
-吧 (ba) [particle] (suggestion)
