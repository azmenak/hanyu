## *intermediate* level

### Pinyin and Translation

女: 嗓子不舒服我们都用
    sǎngzi bù shūfu wǒmen dōu yòng
    Is your throat sore, we all use....

男: 咽喉疼痛,嗓子干涩,请用
    yānhóu téngtòng , sǎngzi gānsè , qǐng yòng
    Sore and dry throat, please use....

男: 金猴子嗓宝
    jīnhóuzi sǎng bǎo
    Golden Monkey Throat Lozenge

客户: 哪儿有卖的?
      nar3 yǒu mài de ?
      Where can I buy it?

男: 全国各大药店均有销售
    quánguó gè dà yàodiàn jūn yǒu xiāoshòu
    For sale at all major pharmacies nationwide

客户: 电话呢?
      diànhuà ne ?
      and the phone number?

女: 800-820-8820
    bālínglíng - bāèrlíng - bābāèrlíng
    800-820-8820
### Vocab
-泡 (pào) [verb] to spend time at

-盘 (pán) [measure word] (measure word for a round in a game)
-人质 (rénzhì) [noun] hostage
-见证 (jiànzhèng) [verb] to testify to
-伟大 (wěidà) [adjective] great
-原来 (yuánlái) [adverb] originally
-趴下 (pāxià) [verb phrase] to get down
-匪 (fěi) [noun] bandit
