# Dude looks like a Lady
## *intermediate* level

### Chinese Text
A：哎，你看那男的多帅啊！
B：帅什么呀，长得跟个女的似的。
A：你不懂，今年就流行这样的！

### Pinyin and Translation

A: 哎，你看那男的多帅啊！
    ài ， nǐ kàn nà nánde duō shuài à ！
    Hey, look at how handsome that guy is!

B: 帅什么呀，长得跟个女的似的。
    shuài shénme yà ， zhǎngde gēn ge nǚde shìde 。
    Handsome? He looks like a girl.

A: 你不懂，今年就流行这样的！
    nǐ bù dǒng ， jīnnián jiù liúxíng zhèyang de ！
    You don't understand. That's in fashion this year!
### Vocab
-男的 (nánde) [noun] man
-多帅 (duō shuài) [phrase] how very handsome
-长得 (zhǎngde) [verb phrase] to resemble
-女的 (nǚde) [noun] woman
-似的 (shìde) [other] like
-不懂 (bù dǒng) [verb] not to understand
-流行 (liúxíng) [adjective] popular
