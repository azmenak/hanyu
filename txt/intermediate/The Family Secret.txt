# The Family Secret
## *intermediate* level

### Chinese Text
爸爸：亲爱的，我觉得是时候谈谈你的儿子了。
妈妈：噢，你还在为那个小事故生气吗？
爸爸：小事故？那可是五百伏电压！
妈妈：噢，怪不得我们家电费这么贵！
爸爸：我的心跳都停了！
妈妈：啊，小孩子，真淘气！

### Pinyin and Translation

爸爸: 亲爱的，我觉得是时候谈谈你的儿子了。
      qīn`ài de ， wǒ juéde shìshíhou tántan nǐ de érzi le 。
      Honey, I think it's about time we talked about your son.

妈妈: 噢，你还在为那个小事故生气吗？
      o ， nǐ hái zài wèi nà ge xiǎo shìgu shēngqì ma ？
      Oh, you're still angry about that little accident?

爸爸: 小事故？那可是五百伏电压！
      xiǎo shìgu ？ nà kě shì wǔ bǎi fú diànyā ！
      Little accident? That was 500 volts of electric current!

妈妈: 噢，怪不得我们家电费这么贵！
      o ， guàibudé wǒmen jiā diànfèi zhème guì ！
      Really? No wonder our electric bill is so high.

爸爸: 我的心跳都停了！
      wǒ de xīntiào dōu tíng le ！
      It stopped my heart!

妈妈: 啊，小孩子，真淘气！
      a ， xiǎo háizi ， zhēn táoqì ！
      Oh, boys will be boys!
### Vocab
-亲爱的 (qīn'ài de) [noun] darling
-两天 (liǎng tiān) [phrase] a few days
-隔壁邻居 (gébì línjū) [noun] neighbor
-只不过 (zhǐ bùguò) [conjunction] it's only that
-难道 (nándào) [conjunction] can it be that
-相互 (xiānghù) [adverb] each other
-谈 (tán) [verb] to discuss
-照顾 (zhàogu) [verb] to take care of
-亲生的 (qīnshēng de) [verb] to give birth oneself
-人家 (rénjia) [pronoun] them
-远亲不如近邻 (yuǎnqīn bùrú jìnlín) [phrase] distant relatives are not as good as close neighbors
