# Chinese Drinking Games
## *intermediate* level

### Chinese Text
甲：猜丁壳！
乙：等会儿，你那是什么呀？
甲：剪刀啊....
乙：有你那样的剪刀吗？你在哪儿学的猜丁壳啊？你小儿麻痹吧你！

### Pinyin and Translation

甲: 猜丁壳！
    cèidīngké ！
    Paper, rock, scissors!

乙: 等会儿，你那是什么呀？
    děnghuìr ， nǐ nà shì shénme ya ？
    Wait a sec, what on earth is that?

甲: 剪刀啊....
    jiǎndāo a . . . .
    Scissors....

乙: 有你那样的剪刀吗？你在哪儿学的猜丁壳啊？你小儿麻痹吧你！
    yǒu nǐ nàyàng de jiǎndāo ma ？ nǐ zài nǎr xué de cèidīngké a ？ nǐ xiǎoérmábì ba nǐ ！
    Are there any scissors that look like that? Where did you learn paper, rock, scissors? Did you fall on your head as a child or something?
### Vocab
-猜丁壳 (cèidīngké) [phrase] paper, rock, scissors
-剪刀 (jiǎndāo) [noun] scissors
-石头 (shítou) [noun] rock
-布 (bù) [noun] cloth
-小儿麻痹 (xiǎoérmábì) [noun] cerebral infantile palsy
-单人我倒霉 (dānrén wǒ dǎoméi) [phrase] I'm the unlucky one
-捉迷藏 (zhuōmícáng) [noun] hide-and-seek
-划拳 (huáquán) [verb] to play a drinking game
-猜拳 (cāiquán) [verb] to play a drinking game
-套话 (tàohuà) [noun] tired phrase
-蛤蟆 (háma) [noun] toad
