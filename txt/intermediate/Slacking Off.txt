# Slacking Off
## *intermediate* level

### Chinese Text
女：咳，老板走了没?
男：终于走了。
女：哎呀，太好了。走。咱们吃饭去!
男：你还没工作完呢?
女：哎呀，老板都走了谁还要工作呢? 走吧, 走吧, 别假正经了!
男：好，走吧。

### Pinyin and Translation

女: 咳，老板走了没?
    hai ， lǎobǎn zǒu le méi ?
    Hey, has the boss gone yet?

男: 终于走了。
    zhōngyú zǒu le 。
    He's finally gone.

女: 哎呀，太好了。走。咱们吃饭去!
    āiya ， tài hǎo le 。 zǒu 。 zánmen chīfàn qù !
    Great! Come on, let's go eat!

男: 你还没工作完呢?
    nǐ hái méi gōngzuò wán ne ?
    Are you finished with work?

女: 哎呀，老板都走了谁还要工作呢? 走吧, 走吧, 别假正经了!
    āiya ， lǎobǎn dōu zǒu le shéi hái yào gōngzuò ne ? zǒu ba , zǒu ba , bié jiǎ zhèngjǐng le !
    What are you thinking? Who keeps working once the boss leaves? Get moving, get moving, don't act all straightlaced!

男: 好，走吧。
    hǎo ， zǒu ba 。
    Ok, let's go.
### Vocab
-走了没 (zǒu le méi) [verb phrase] to have left or not
-终于 (zhōngyú) [adverb] at last
-哎呀 (āiyā) [exclamation] good gracious
-咱们 (zánmen) [pronoun] we
-都 (dōu) [adverb] (all)
-别 (bié) [auxiliary verb] don't
-正经 (zhèngjǐng) [adjective] proper
-假正经 (jiǎ zhèngjǐng) [verb phrase] to pretend to be straight and narrow
