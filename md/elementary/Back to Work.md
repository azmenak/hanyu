# Back to Work
## *elementary* level

### Chinese Text
甲：这个怎么说？
甲：笑什么你？
乙：你刚跑出东北味来了。

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|

|甲|这个怎么说？|
||zhège zěnme shuō ？|
||How do you say this?|

|甲|笑什么你？|
||xiào shénme nǐ ？|
||What are you laughing at?|

|乙|你刚跑出东北味来了。|
||nǐ gāng pǎochū dōngběiwèi lái le 。|
||You just let slip a Northeastern accent.|
