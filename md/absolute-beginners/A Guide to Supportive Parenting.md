# A Guide to Supportive Parenting
**Absolute Beginners** level
### Chinese Text
男：你是谁？
女：我是你的老婆！

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|
|男|你是谁？<blockquote>nǐ shì shéi ？<br />Who are you?</blockquote>|
|女|我是你的老婆！<blockquote>wǒ shì nǐ de lǎopo ！<br />I'm your wife!</blockquote>|
