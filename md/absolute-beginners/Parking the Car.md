# Parking the Car
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：先生，请系好安全套。
乙：啊？你说什么？
甲：安全带，安全带。

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|

|甲|先生，请系好安全套。|
||xiānsheng ， qǐng jìhǎo ānquántào 。|
||Mister. Please put on your condom.|

|乙|啊？你说什么？|
||a ？ nǐ shuō shénme ？|
||What? What did you say?|

|甲|安全带，安全带。|
||ānquándài ， ānquándài 。|
||Seatbelt, seatbelt....|
