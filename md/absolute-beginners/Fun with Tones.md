# Fun with Tones
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
A：我爱你.
B：你爱我,可是我爱他.
A：他爱你吗?
B：他爱你!

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|

|A|我爱你.|
||wǒ ài nǐ .|
||I love you.|

|B|你爱我,可是我爱他.|
||nǐ ài wǒ , kěshì wǒ ài tā .|
||You love me, but I love him.|

|A|他爱你吗?|
||tā ài nǐ ma ?|
||Does he love you?|

|B|他爱你!|
||tā ài nǐ !|
||He loves you|
