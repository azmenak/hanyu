# Sometimes you just can't take it anymore....
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：停下。
甲：够了，够了。
甲：住手！

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|
|甲|停下。<br />tíngxià 。<br />Stop.|
|甲|够了，够了。<br />gòule ， gòule 。<br />Enough, enough.|
|甲|住手！<br />zhù shǒu ！<br />Stop it!|
