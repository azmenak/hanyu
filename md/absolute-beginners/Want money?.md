# Want money?
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：几点了？
乙：两点。
甲：天哪！晚了，晚了！

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|

|甲|几点了？|
||jǐ diǎn le ？|
||What time is it?|

|乙|两点。|
||liǎng diǎn 。|
||Two o'clock.|

|甲|天哪！晚了，晚了！|
||tiānna ！ wǎn le ， wǎn le ！|
||Good heavens! It's late!|
