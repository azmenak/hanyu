# The Secret Lives of Celebrities
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：几点关门儿？
乙：我们不关门儿。
甲：是吗？
乙：对，我们通宵。

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|

|甲|几点关门儿？|
||jǐdiǎn guān ménr ？|
||What time do you close?|

|乙|我们不关门儿。|
||wǒmen bù guān ménr 。|
||We don't close.|

|甲|是吗？|
||shìma ？|
||Is that the case?|

|乙|对，我们通宵。|
||duì ， wǒmen tōngxiāo 。|
||Correct, we're open all night.|
