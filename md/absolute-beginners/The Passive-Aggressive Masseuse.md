# The Passive-Aggressive Masseuse
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
一：你觉得_____怎么样？
二：我觉得______不错。
一：我同意。
二：好，明天见！

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|

|一|你觉得_____怎么样？|
||nǐ juéde _____ zěnmeyàng ？|
||How you you feel about _____?|

|二|我觉得______不错。|
||wǒ juéde ______ bùcuò 。|
||I think _____ isn't bad.|

|一|我同意。|
||wǒ tóngyì 。|
||I agree.|

|二|好，明天见！|
||hǎo ， míng tiān jiàn ！|
||Ok, see you tomorrow!|
