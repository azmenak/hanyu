# The Unsuccessful Haggle
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
男：谁放屁啦？
女：不是我。
男：很臭啊。
女：是你吧！

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|

|男|谁放屁啦？|
||shéi fàngpì la ？|
||Who farted?|

|女|不是我。|
||bù shì wǒ 。|
||It wasn't me.|

|男|很臭啊。|
||hěn chòu a 。|
||That really smells.|

|女|是你吧！|
||shì nǐ ba ！|
||It was you!|
