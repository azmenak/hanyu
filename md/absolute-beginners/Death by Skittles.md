# Death by Skittles
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
甲：哦，彩虹糖！
甲：我吃一个。
甲：真好吃。
甲：谢谢啊！

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|

|甲|哦，彩虹糖！|
||ò ， cǎihóngtáng ！|
||Oh, Skittles!|

|甲|我吃一个。|
||wǒ chī yī ge 。|
||I'll have one.|

|甲|真好吃。|
||zhēn hǎochī 。|
||Really delicious.|

|甲|谢谢啊！|
||xièxiè ā ！|
||Thanks!|
