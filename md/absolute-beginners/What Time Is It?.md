# What Time Is It?
## *absolute-beginners* level

### Chinese Text
男：你真漂亮。
女：你真帅。
男：你真聪明。
女：你真... 帅。

### Pinyin and Translation
|说人|句子|
|----|----|

|男|你真漂亮。|
||nǐ zhēn piàoliang 。|
||You're really pretty.|

|女|你真帅。|
||nǐ zhēn shuài 。|
||You're really handsome.|

|男|你真聪明。|
||nǐ zhēn cōngming 。|
||You're really smart.|

|女|你真... 帅。|
||nǐ zhēn ... shuài 。|
||You're really... handsome.|
